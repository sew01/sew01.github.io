OUR PRODUCTS

Lathe Machine

This is a machine that rotates a workpiece about an axis of rotation to perform various operations with tools that are applied to the workpiece to create an object with symmetry.

Rigid Stranding Machine

This machine is used for stranding copper, steel and aluminium, stranding unilay and reverse concentric.

Rewinding Lines

This is engineered to wind wires or insulated cables from one reel size/type to a different reel size/type in order to prepare the product for a different internal process and packaging.

Drum Twister Machine

This machine is widely used for effective cabling of round and sectored power cables. In addition to this, it is also used for closing of stranded conductors.
